DESCRIPTION

Pakistani cuisine is a vibrant mix of bold flavors, aromatic spices, and diverse regional influences. Known for its hearty curries, flavorful biryanis, and sizzling kebabs, it blends Mughal, Persian, and Central Asian culinary traditions. Dishes like nihari, karahi, and haleem showcase the love for slow-cooked meals, while street food delights like samosas and chaat add a burst of tangy and spicy flavors. Accompanied by naan or roti and complemented with fresh chutneys and raita, Pakistani food is a feast for the senses.
