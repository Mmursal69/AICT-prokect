Malai Tikka Boti

Malai Tikka Boti

INGREDIENTS :

500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
1/2 cup cream
2 tbsp yogurt
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp white pepper powder
1/2 tsp garam masala powder
2 tbsp lemon juice
Salt to taste

INSTRUCTIONS :

In a bowl, mix cream, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, white pepper, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt.
Add the chicken cubes and marinate for at least 2 hours (preferably overnight for better flavor).
Skewer the marinated chicken cubes and grill on medium heat until fully cooked, turning occasionally.
Serve hot with naan, paratha, or rice, and a side of mint chutney.
