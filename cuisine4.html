Seekh Kebab

Seekh Kebab

INGREDIENTS :

500g minced beef or mutton
1 medium onion (finely chopped)
2 tbsp gram flour (roasted)
1 tsp red chili flakes
1 tsp cumin powder
1/2 tsp garam masala
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
Salt to taste

INSTRUCTIONS :

Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix thoroughly.
Refrigerate the mixture for 1 hour to firm it up.
Shape the mixture around skewers to form long kebabs.
Grill or pan-fry the kebabs until cooked through, turning occasionally.
Serve with mint chutney and onion rings.
