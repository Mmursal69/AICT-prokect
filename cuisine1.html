Mutton karahi

Mutton karahi

INGREDIENTS :

1 kg mutton (bone-in)
4 medium tomatoes (chopped)
1 medium onion (finely chopped)
1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
1/2 cup yogurt
1 tbsp red chili powder
1 tsp turmeric powder
2-3 green chilies (slit)
1 tsp garam masala powder
1/2 cup cooking oil
Fresh coriander (chopped) for garnish
Salt to taste

INSTRUCTIONS :

Heat oil in a wok or karahi and fry the chopped onions until golden brown.
Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
Add the mutton pieces, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Cook until the meat changes color.
Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften, stirring frequently.
Add the yogurt and continue cooking until the oil separates from the masala.
Stir in the green chilies and garam masala, cover, and simmer until the mutton becomes tender. Add water as needed to prevent burning.
Garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot with naan or chapati.
